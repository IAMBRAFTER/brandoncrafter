BIOGRAPHY

Brandon Crafter is an entrepreneur, investor, father, and husband with a lifelong interest in business and branding. With a background in marketing and a passion for helping individuals find their authentic selves, he brings over twelve years of industry experience to his work. Throughout his career, Brandon has collaborated with clients from diverse backgrounds, gaining a deep understanding of the unique challenges faced by underrepresented communities. As a branding consultant and advocate, he strives to create equitable access to business and marketing education and resources, empowering others to achieve their goals.

Career

Brandon started his first business, a branding and design agency, at the age of 27. The agency quickly gained traction and Brandon was able to grow it into a successful company with clients all over the country.

Along the way, he also started investing in stocks, startups and eventually real estate, which helped him build his wealth and diversify his portfolio. As Brandon's reputation grew, he started getting more and more requests to speak at conferences and events. He quickly realized that he had a talent for public speaking and decided to add "influencer" to his list of titles.

Now, Brandon spends his time running his businesses, coaching other entrepreneurs on branding and marketing, and sharing his knowledge and insights with his community.

Personality

Brandon is a natural leader who is confident, articulate, and charismatic. He is always looking for new opportunities and loves to take risks. He is also a great communicator and has a talent for breaking down complex concepts into easy-to-understand ideas.

Despite his success, Brandon is down-to-earth and approachable. He genuinely enjoys helping others and is always willing to lend a hand to those who are just starting out. He believes that everyone has the potential to be successful if they are willing to put in the work.
